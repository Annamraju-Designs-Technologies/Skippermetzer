About Us

Skipper Metzer India LLP is an Indo-Israeli joint venture between M/s Skipper Limited, India and M/s Metzer, Israel

to foray into Micro Irrigation market in India with an objective to become a key player and bring in new technologies in the growing irrigation market to help farmers. Skipper Metzer Head Office and production facilities with state of the art "Israeli Technologies" are located in Hyderabad. Skipper Metzer is led by experienced team members with more than two and half decades of experience in Micro Irrigation sector on PAN India basis and backup of world class Israeli Technology. Skipper Metzer has established manufacturing capacities for the full range of Drip Irrigation and Sprinkler Irrigation Systems to support Indian Farmers with latest technology & help them to improve their yields, productivity & income.

Skipper Metzer is registered supplier in major states in India under the Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (known as PMKSY) to offer our Micro Irrigation Systems to eligible farmers. The company has established offices & warehouses at strategic locations and duly supported by a network of large number of channel partners.


We have set up the most comprehensive quality control lab at our production facilities with the complete quality testing equipment for offering the word class products for the farmers. Moreover we have backup of the strong R&D from our promoters company (M/s Metzerplas Israel).
